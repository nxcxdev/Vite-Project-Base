Wheel of Witch

About the game

There's a witch among the citizens. Each night, the witch will choose a citizen to offer as sacrifice.

How to play

Add as much players as you want to the game. Then, press ¨Start Game¨ to start playing.

Each round, a player will be randomly eliminated.
